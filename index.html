Saad Gulzar


Associate Professor of Political Science
Associate Professor of Global Affairs
Associate Professor of Economics (Concurrent)

University of Notre Dame
CV Email Scholar
My research interests lie in political economy, comparative politics, development, and environmental governance and conservation, with a regional focus on South Asia. I received my Ph.D. in Politics from New York University in 2017. Previously, I was on the faculty of Stanford University and Princeton University.
